About

A company built
from the inside out

Silk Ivory is an artist management company, boutique record imprint, and production house based in Los Angeles.

Founded by Adam "4th Pyramid" Farag — a Toronto-born, LA-based producer, songwriter, and DJ with over two decades of experience at the highest levels of the music industry — Silk Ivory operates as a full-service creative and business infrastructure for artists who are ready to build something real.

What sets Silk Ivory apart is the rare combination of creative and executive capability under one roof. Our founder has been in the room where the music is made and in the room where the deals are signed. That dual fluency shapes how we approach every artist relationship.

We don't manage volume. We manage with depth — working closely with a selective roster to build careers that compound over time.

Founder

Adam "4th Pyramid" Farag

Adam Farag is a producer, songwriter, mix engineer and DJ whose work spans hip-hop, R&B, indie, and beyond. His production and co-writing credits include 21 Savage, The Black Eyed Peas, EARTHGANG, Wu-Tang Clan, MF DOOM, BigXThaPlug, Allan Rayman and many others.

In 2024, he co-wrote 21 Savage's "Red Sky" which peaked at #57 on the Billboard Hot 100, appearing on American Dream — which debuted at #1 on the Billboard 200. In 2023, his production on Tommy Newport's "Sunset For The Dead" peaked at #15 on Spotify's Viral Top 50.

As a manager, Adam signed Tommy Newport in 2018 and guided him from independent artist to major label deal, accumulating 100M+ streams, placements in film and television, and co-signs from Zane Lowe, Hypebeast, The FADER, COLORS and NYLON along the way.
