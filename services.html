Services

What we do
and how we do it

01

Artist Management

Full-service management for artists at the development and growth stage. We handle strategy, label relations, deal negotiation, touring infrastructure, creative direction, and day-to-day operations — so artists can focus on the work.

  • Career strategy & long-term planning
  • Label deal negotiation & A&R relationships
  • Release strategy & rollout planning
  • Brand partnerships & sync opportunities
  • Team building & contractor management
02

Music Production

Production led by Adam "4th Pyramid" Farag — a multi-genre producer with credits across hip-hop, R&B, indie, and pop. We work across full project executive production and individual records.

  • Executive production & A&R
  • Beat production & co-production
  • Songwriting & toplining
  • Mixing & engineering
  • Session coordination
03

Imprint & Label Services

Silk Ivory operates as a boutique imprint with established distributor and label relationships. For artists ready to release independently or through a partner, we provide the infrastructure and relationships to do it properly.

  • Distribution partnerships (AWAL, Stem, Vydia & others)
  • Label deal structuring & pitching
  • Independent release infrastructure
  • Sync licensing & placement
  • Publishing administration
04

Sync & Licensing

A proven track record of music placement in film, television, and advertising. We actively pitch catalog and new recordings to music supervisors across all major networks, studios, and brands.

  • Film & television sync pitching
  • Advertising & brand campaigns
  • Catalog licensing
  • Trailer & promo music

We work with a small number of artists at a time.
If you think there's a fit, let's talk.
