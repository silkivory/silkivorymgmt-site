Silk Ivory is a Los Angeles-based management firm, record imprint, and production house founded by Platinum producer Adam "4th Pyramid" Farag.

We provide the creative and business architecture for a highly curated roster of artists and producers to scale. Built on twenty years of production and executive experience, we prioritize long-term brand equity over volume.

At Silk Ivory, we bridge the gap between creative execution and high-level strategy. Our roster includes Platinum and Gold-certified talent with a global footprint in sync, securing placements with brands like Apple, Nike, HBO, NFL, Sprite and Disney, and building sustainable career paths that compound over time.
